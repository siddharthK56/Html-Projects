Project-1: Textual Elegance - HTML Formatting Mastery

Problem Statement :

You are tasked with creating a web page that showcases your proficiency in HTML formatting. The goal is to demonstrate your ability to structure content and apply various HTML elements to achieve a visually appealing and well-formatted webpage. Your webpage should include textual content, images, links, and other HTML elements to showcase the versatility of HTML in creating aesthetically pleasing textual content.

Requirements

  • Content Structurre :

    • Create a webpage with at least two distinct sections, such as an introduction and a main content area.

    • Use appropriate HTML structural elements like <_header> ,<_Main>,<_Section> and <_Footer>

  • Textual Formatting :

    • Apply different text formatting elements, such as <_h1> to <_h6>, <_p>, <_strong>, <_em> and <_blockquote>

    • Experiment with text alignment using attributes like text-align.

  • Lists and Tables
    • Include an ordered or unordered list with at least three list items.
    • Create a table with at least three rows and three columns, incorporating headers and data cells
  • Hyperlinks and Images
    • Add hyperlinks to external websites and internal sections of your web page.
    • Embed at least two images with appropriate alt text
